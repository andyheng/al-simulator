          This simulator makes certain assumptions about how the game works:

          • While the contents of the permanent pools (Light Construction, Heavy Construction, Special Construction) are known, no exact data is provided or known about the rates of each inidividual ship. Therefore, ships with no given rate (ie. not on rate-up) are randomly chosen.
          • While it is known that each limited pool uses ships from a certain permanent pool (usually heavy or special), it is not known exactly which ships are used. Generally, from user experience, it has been observed that not all ships in permanent pools are included in limited pools (ie. a limited pool that pulls from the special pool may exclude certain permanent SSR ships that have been added recently). Therefore, this simulator uses slightly different datasets when constructing from permanent pools and from limited pools.

          These assumptions may make this simulator not completely accurate to the game, but they act as a bandaid without total information of the rates and content.
